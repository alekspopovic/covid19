About

Covid-19 Historical Data is a simple dashboard for tracking and comparing historical data for Covid-19 virus.

All country data is being fetched via NovelCOVID API.

All news, emergencies and outbreaks data is being fetched via World Health Organization's official website and WHO's rss feed.
